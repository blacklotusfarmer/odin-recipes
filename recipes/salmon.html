Salmon Recipe

Description

A quick and easy air-friend salmon recipe when you're low on time, but want a good meal.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) frozen salmon fillets

Steps

  1. Preheat to 390 degrees F (190 degrees C). Spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray.
  2. Mix Dijon, honey, balsamic vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Place the frozen salmon in the preheated air fryer and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Brush the mixture over the salmon and cook it until it flakes easily with a fork.
